Proof of attendance

  • Proof of attendance

    Through the button below, you can prove your daily attendance if you are already a member of the team.

  • Proof of absence

    If there is any illness or decision not to attend, you must prove the absence from below and write a note or the reason for your absence from the day.

  • Late attendance request

    If you want to prove your attendance but you will be late than the normal time, you can create a proof of attendance and write the reason for your lateness and the time of your arrival in the notes box..
